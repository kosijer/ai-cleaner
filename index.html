How is the AI Likelihood Score calculated?

The AI Likelihood Score analyzes multiple factors to determine how likely text is to be AI-generated:

  • Special Characters (20%): Unicode symbols, mathematical operators, etc.
  • Language Patterns (30%): Formal phrases, academic language, transition words
  • Structural Patterns (25%): Consistent sentence lengths, uniform paragraphs, lists
  • Vocabulary (15%): Sophisticated words, technical jargon
  • Repetition (10%): Overused words, repetitive sentence starters

Note: Scores above 70% suggest high likelihood of AI generation, while scores below 30% suggest human-written text.
